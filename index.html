Biography

I am a PhD research student at University of New South Wales - Canberra. I started reading my PhD on July 2016 in the area of "Usable Security and Privacy". My research focuses on improving usability of security APIs.

My other research interests include Identity and Access Management, Machine Learning, Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence.

I received a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science & Engineering from University of Moratuwa in 2015 with First Class distinction.
